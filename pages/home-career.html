Who Are We

We are a global Offshore outsourcing company which is headquartered in New York with offices in Ahmedabad, India. We are a certified member of American Staffing Association and New York Staffing Association. We are also ISO 27001 and 9001:2015 certified.

Our Mission

Our mission is to be excellent at everything that we do. We aim to serve our clients with comprehensive, efficient and cost-effective solutions that are in alignment with their core business goals.

What We Do
