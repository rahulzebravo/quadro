Why are small and mid-sized staffing companies exploring RPO?

Leave a Comments / RPO / By Admin

Recruitment process outsourcing (RPO) is exactly what it sounds like: a company outsources the recruitment to an external agency to get offshore personnel who can assist the onshore team. By outsourcing this work, onshore staff can focus on the internal matters of the company.

Explore the reasons why many small to mid-sized staffing companies are opting for this recruitment solution.

Take a look at the challenges listed below and evaluate – is your company going through the same issues and you haven’t recognised it yet? Our expert team has offered a solution for each issue. Reach out to us and let us know if this article helped you.

The Challenge

It is quite a task to navigate the labour market to single out the talent you are looking for. It is necessary to have a set of skills and experts working on the task to ensure you find the person you are looking for. Further, it is necessary for a recruiter to think beyond the talent itself.

There is a necessity for them to meet hiring manager satisfaction, managing candidate experience, reporting overall performances, and maintaining scalability needs.

How can these challenges be navigated?

Enters RPO

Recruitment process outsourcing firms answer all these questions. An RPO is a source-agnostic body that sources candidates according to the company’s referral programs and through major platforms actively and passively.

RPOs also maintain Cost-Effectiveness on all fronts. This means being mindful of all the additional costs and more efficient alternatives. For example, hiring an on-shore resource brings up a number of infrastructure costs which you might not be ready for. However, this requirement is eliminated with an RPO.

Method and functioning

RPOs work on recruiting talent for a company by following a specific guideline. The details may vary but they all essentially include the factors mentioned below:

Analysis

RPOs study the market to find out the needs of the business and the position they are recruiting for. This helps them understand the complete requirements the position calls for. They are especially beneficial for SMEs as they save a lot of time and cost which has more pull in smaller businesses.

Engaging talent

RPOs also function to help an employer branding in terms of employment and recognition in the labour market. This generally happens through communities where the RPO engages talent in advance.

Assessment

RPOs ensure that only the best talent is recruited through their services. They assess candidates based on their skills, motivators, and competencies.

The Benefits

As promised, take a look at some of these better-than-magic advantages that come with hiring an RPO.

They seem like a magical fix, but the benefits a company reaps by hiring a recruitment process outsourcing company is a little better than magic.

When you work with an RPO, you can expect to:

-Manage the hiring fluctuations throughout the year without losing precious time or resources.

-Improve hiring manager satisfaction so they can cross it off their long list!

-Get started instantly when you work with RPOs whereas hiring someone in a team generally takes up to 20 days.

-Get on a monthly rollover contract: RPOs usually have these so you don’t need to commit long term. Hiring locally means you commit to employee-related taxes and benefits.

-Maintain the work culture in your environment, without the dynamics shifting with every change in personnel.

-Elevate employment brands with RPOs as they act as an extension of your business and generate awareness.

-Improve candidate experience because RPOs at their crux are human resource wizards.

-Simplify the hiring process so there is one less issue to comb through when doing the hectic job of running a company.

-Work with experts at recruitment, and learn the secrets of the industry so your business can grow.

-Get access to major job boards and save 60% overall on investment that an onshore hire requires.

An RPO can essentially save time, save money, and get you the best candidates – while making your hiring manager smile a lot more.
