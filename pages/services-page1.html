Why Outsource Recruitment Process?

Career Builder conducted a survey of 2700 employers in 2011 according to which 41% of the employers estimated that a single bad hire costed them more than $25,000 and 25% said that a single bad hire costed them more than $50,000.

Recruitment Process Outsourcing is a form of Business Process Outsourcing (BPO) where the employer transfers either all or part of its recruitment processes to an external provider. RPO providers assume ownership of the design and management of the recruitment process and the responsibility of results. RPO helps boost employer’s brand and find the best talent possible, instead of simply filling positions and finding people who are qualified.
